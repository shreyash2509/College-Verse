About CollegeVerse

CollegeVerse - brought to you by Apni Academy is here to provide counseling services to all Engineering and Medical Aspirants. CollegeVerse has become a trademark and is the first choice of the students for counseling-related services due to the exceptional results and feedback from the previous batches. For Engineering, we provide counseling services for JEE (JoSAA), WBJEE, COMEDK, JAC Chandigarh, JAC Delhi, MHT-CET, and various other Government and Deemed Universities. For Medical Aspirants we have counseling services for NEET and admission to various other Foreign and Deemed Universities.

Moreover, CollegeVerse is here to provide the most important services for students during the competitive exams. Like providing Last minute Revision Notes, DPP, Mind Map, Previous year Question Papers, etc. We want to provide the students with a proper examination environment, So we are also providing Mock Test feature for various examinations like JEE, COMEDK, WBJEE, etc
